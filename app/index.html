DIY Paper Airplane

Step 1:

Fold the paper in half

Step 2:

Unfold, and then fold the corners into the center line

Step 3:

Fold the top edges to the center

Step 4:

Fold the paper airplane in half

Step 5:

Fold the wings down to meet the bottom edge of the plane's body

Step 6:

This airplane has stiff wings, so it can tolerate fast throwing speeds. Throw it gently but in a quick for maximum speed and distance
